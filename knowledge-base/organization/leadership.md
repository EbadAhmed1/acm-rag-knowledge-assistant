## Structure
The society operates with a main Executive Committee and an Extended Executive Committee for the 2025-2026 tenure.

# Extended Executive Committee Teams

The Extended Executive Committee consists of multiple specialized teams:

Branding: Manages the society's image and visual identity.

Technology: Oversees technical aspects of events and projects.

Marketing: Promotes events and activities.

Guest Relations: Handles invitations and logistics for guest speakers and attendees.

SOP Compliance: Ensures adherence to standard operating procedures.

Event Administration: Manages organizational details of events.

EM Competitions: Focuses on competition management and execution.

Event Management: Oversees planning and execution of events.

Public Relations: Manages public image and communications.

Promotions: Handles promotional campaigns.

Creativity: Develops creative ideas and concepts.

Content: Produces written and visual content.

Media: Manages media coverage and presence.

Design: Creates graphic design and visual material.

In-House: Manages internal affairs and member engagement.

Animations: Produces animated promotional content.

Automations: Develops automated processes for efficiency.

Web Development: Maintains website and web-based projects.

Tech Competitions: Organizes technology-focused competitions.

Tech Operations: Handles technical logistics during events.

UI/UX Design: Works on user interface and experience for digital projects.
